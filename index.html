The Problem

Farmers lose nearly 15% of their crops to bird attacks every season. Traditional fixes like scarecrows, plates, and noise-making tools stop working quickly because birds are smart — they adapt to repetitive sounds within days, making manual methods a waste of time and energy.

🌾 Crop Damage

Birds destroy seeds, fruits and grains during critical growth stages, leading to significant losses before harvest.

💪 Manual Effort

Farmers spend hours every day manually scaring birds using plates, sticks and noise — time that could be spent elsewhere.

🔄 Birds Adapt

Sound-based deterrents become completely useless within days as birds learn that the noise poses no real threat.

Our Smart Solution

AgroShield installs sensor poles around the perimeter of your field. When a bird is detected, targeted water jets fire instantly — scaring birds away while also watering your crops. Simple, smart, sustainable.
